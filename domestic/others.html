Domestic Pest Control for Other Pests

Welsh Town and Country Pest Services provides expert domestic pest control for a range of other pests across Neath, Port Talbot, Swansea, and South Wales. Our services cover nuisance wildlife such as squirrels, foxes, birds, and moles. Our skilled team uses effective techniques to address and manage these pests, ensuring that your home remains safe and secure. From preventing wildlife entry to removing unwanted pests, we offer tailored solutions to meet your specific needs and restore peace to your living environment.

Squirrel Control

Our squirrel control services focus on managing and removing squirrels that may invade your home or garden. We use effective traps and deterrents to address squirrel infestations and prevent future problems, ensuring your property remains pest-free.

Fox Control

Welsh Town and Country Pest Services offers comprehensive fox control solutions to manage foxes that may cause disturbances or damage. We employ humane and effective methods to safely remove foxes and prevent them from returning to your property.

Bird Control

Our bird control services address issues with nuisance birds, such as pigeons and seagulls. We use a range of strategies, including deterrents and exclusion methods, to manage and control bird populations and protect your property.

Mole Control

For effective mole control, we provide targeted treatments to manage and remove moles that may cause damage to lawns and gardens. Our expert team uses proven methods to address mole infestations and restore the appearance of your outdoor spaces.
