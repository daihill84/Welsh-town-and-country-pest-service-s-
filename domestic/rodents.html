Domestic pest control

At Welsh Town and Country Pest Services, we provide expert domestic rodent control in Neath, Port Talbot, Swansea, and South Wales. Our services are designed to address all rodent issues, including mice and rats, ensuring your home remains pest-free. We use a range of effective methods, including thorough inspections, targeted trapping, and baiting systems, to eliminate rodent infestations and prevent future problems. Trust our experienced team to deliver safe and reliable rodent control solutions tailored to your specific needs.

Mouse Control

Our mouse control services include comprehensive inspections to identify entry points, targeted trapping, and baiting to eliminate mice. We ensure your home is free from mice and protected against future infestations.

Rat Control

We offer specialized rat control solutions, including advanced baiting systems and trapping methods. Our team works to remove rats from your property and implement preventative measures to avoid future issues.

Rodent Prevention

Our rodent prevention services focus on identifying and sealing entry points, eliminating food sources, and providing ongoing monitoring to ensure your home stays rodent-free.
