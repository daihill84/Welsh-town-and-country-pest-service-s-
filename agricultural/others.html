Comprehensive Agricultural Pest Control Services

Welsh Town and Country Pest Services specializes in managing agricultural pests that can impact your farm's productivity and safety. Our expert team provides targeted solutions for controlling birds, moles, and foxes, ensuring the protection of your crops, livestock, and property across Resolven, Neath, and surrounding areas. We use effective methods to address pest issues and prevent future infestations, offering peace of mind for farmers and landowners.

Bird Control

Our bird control services are designed to protect your agricultural property from damage caused by various bird species. We employ effective strategies to deter birds from feeding on crops, nesting on buildings, and causing other disturbances. Our solutions are tailored to your specific needs to ensure long-term success and minimize bird-related problems.

Mole Control

Moles can cause significant damage to farmland by disrupting soil structure and affecting crop growth. Welsh Town and Country Pest Services offers expert mole control to manage and eliminate moles effectively. Our methods include trapping and preventative measures to protect your fields from mole damage.

Fox Control

Foxes can pose a threat to livestock and property on agricultural land. Our fox control services involve safe and humane methods to manage and remove foxes from your property. We provide comprehensive solutions to prevent fox-related issues and safeguard your farm.
