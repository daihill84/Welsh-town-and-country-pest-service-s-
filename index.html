Domestic Pest Control Services

Welsh Town and Country Pest Services offers expert domestic rodent control in Neath, Port Talbot, Swansea, and South Wales. Our services are designed to tackle all rodent issues, including mice and rats, ensuring your home remains pest-free. We employ effective methods such as thorough inspections, targeted trapping, and baiting systems to eliminate rodent infestations and prevent future problems. Rely on our experienced team to deliver safe and reliable rodent control solutions tailored to your specific needs.

Our Pest Control Services

Mouse Control

Our mouse control services include comprehensive inspections to identify entry points, targeted trapping, and baiting to effectively eliminate mice. We ensure your home is free from mice and protected against future infestations.

Rat Control

We offer specialized rat control solutions, including advanced baiting systems and trapping methods. Our team works diligently to remove rats from your property and implement preventative measures to avoid future issues.

Rodent Prevention

Our rodent prevention services focus on identifying and sealing entry points, eliminating food sources, and providing ongoing monitoring to ensure your home stays rodent-free.
